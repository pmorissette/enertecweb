Our History

Heated Door Thresholds

Key Features

  • Several thousand units in service throughout North America since 2000
  • Heating element designed to last 25 years
  • Thresholds available in stainless steel (fabricated), aluminum (fabricated, cast, or extruded), or cast iron
  • 100% designed and assembled in-house
  • Heating element designed to self-regulate at 125F maximum
  • 100% resistant to water infiltration, exceeding IP67
  • Guaranteed NO HOT SPOTS
  • Custom design for each application based on shape, material, surface finish and wattage

Electrical Bus Bars

Key Features

  • Thousands in service since 2006 throughout North America
  • Made from C110 copper
  • Several plating options depending on requirements (tin, nickel, silver, or gold)
  • Shock-resisting coatings made from nylon or polymer alloy
